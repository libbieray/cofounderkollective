# CoFounder Kollective — Design Brainstorm

## Chosen Direction: Electric Bold Editorial

**Design Movement:** High-impact editorial magazine meets DTC brand identity
**Core Principles:**
1. Hot pink (#FF2D78) as the dominant accent — unapologetic, feminine, powerful
2. Barlow Condensed for all headlines — tight, massive, all-caps authority
3. DM Sans for body — clean, readable, modern
4. White backgrounds with black hero — maximum contrast and visual drama

**Color Philosophy:**
- White (#FFFFFF) — primary background, clean and premium
- Near-black (#111111) — hero section, creates drama
- Hot pink (#FF2D78) — the brand colour, used for CTAs, accents, highlights
- Charcoal (#1A1A1A) — footer, dark sections
- Cream (#FAFAF8) — alternating section backgrounds

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric two-column grids, full-bleed hero, tight editorial spacing

**Signature Elements:**
1. Oversized condensed headlines that break the grid
2. Hot pink CTA blocks and stat cards
3. Animated ticker strip in solid pink

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Barlow Condensed 900 weight, uppercase, tight tracking
- Italic contrast: Cormorant Garamond for "something" and "from scratch" moments
- Body: DM Sans 300/400 weight
